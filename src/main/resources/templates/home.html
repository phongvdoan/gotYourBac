    • What is BAC???
    • Office of Alcohol Policy and Education defines
    • Blood Alcohol Concentration (BAC) as the percent
    • of alcohol (ethyl alcohol or ethanol) in a person's blood stream.
    • A BAC of .10% means that an individual's blood supply contains
    • one part alcohol for every 1000 parts blood.
